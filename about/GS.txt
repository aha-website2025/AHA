Gio Shin is an Associate Land Use Planner at Pattern Planning & Development Inc., where he works on urban planning, housing, and entitlement projects throughout Los Angeles and Southern California. He holds a Master of Architecture from the Harvard Graduate School of Design and a Master of Public Health from Washington University in St. Louis. With a background that bridges design, public health, and planning, his work focuses on how spatial, social, and environmental systems intersect to shape equitable and sustainable urban development.
